CM Punk Smashes T-Shirt Record On Pro Wrestling Tees

July 18, 2024
blog

If you still don’t think is over with fans, check out this new stat courtesy of Pro Wrestling Tees.

It has been revealed that CM Punk has broken the record for most T-Shirts sold within a 24 hour span. The previous holder of the record was Brodie Lee in a tie with Sting.

The shirt was originally sold in a ‘ringer style’ which has black cuffs and a black neckline but was completely wiped out of inventory. This shirt was available for $29.99. The new version of the shirt currently on sale is $24.99 and does not have the black cuff/neckline style but instead is just all white. Pro Wrestling Tees said “Rather than completely putting the product out of stock, we are now offering it to you on a white t-shirt in many different styles. Ringer tees will still be available at AEW Live Events while supply lasts.”

The website crashed less than 5 minutes after the shirt was posted for sale and went in and out for the next 15 hours as people continued to try and gain access to the site. The site revealed they had to change servers entirely in an effort to stop this from happening again in the future.
