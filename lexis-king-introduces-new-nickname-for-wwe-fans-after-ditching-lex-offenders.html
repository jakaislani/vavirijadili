Lexis King Introduces New Nickname for WWE Fans After Ditching Lex Offenders

July 03, 2024
blog

Brian Pillman Jr’s contract with AEW expired earlier this year and he made his way to the WWE Performance Center. He finally made his way to NXT a while ago and is slowly establishing himself as a star there. Now known as Lexis King, he seems to have a new name for his fans after ditching ‘Lex Offenders.’

Lexis King brought a whole new look after becoming a star in NXT, something that has made him become a subject of ridicule. That’s not all as Lexis King decided to put his foot down regarding what fans call themselves.

During a recent Twitch stream, Lexis King requested his fans to stop calling themselves ‘Registered Lex Offenders.‘ Lexis King took to Twitter and decided to reveal a new name for his fans – Intellexuals.

The NXT Superstar called on his fans to order their own personalized Cameo video to spread the Holiday cheer this season. Whether or not anyone will genuinely want his videos is a different story entirely.

”Calling all of my Intellexuals!!! Show off your intellexuality this holiday season by ordering a cameo for your friends and family! #LongLiveLexisKing”

Lexis King also advanced in the NXT Men’s Breakout Tournament on this past week’s episode of NXT. It remains to be seen how he will continue to be booked as we approach 2024.
