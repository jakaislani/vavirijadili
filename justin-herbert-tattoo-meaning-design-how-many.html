Justin Herbert Tattoo: A Tribute to His Family

According to reports, Justin Herbert has only one tattoo on his body. It is located on his left shoulder blade and is a tribute to his family.

The tattoo features the initials of his parents, Mark and Holly, and his two brothers, Mitchell and Patrick. The tattoo also has “faith” written in cursive below the initials.

Justin Herbert got the tattoo when he was 18, before starting his college career at Oregon.

He said that he wanted to honor his family for their support and guidance throughout his life. The quarterback also said faith is integral to his identity and values.

“I wanted something that would always remind me of them,” Herbert said in an interview with The Athletic. “They’ve been there for me through everything.

“They’ve sacrificed so much for me. And faith is something that I’ve always had. It’s something that I’ve always leaned on.”
