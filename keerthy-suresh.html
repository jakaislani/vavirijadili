Keerthy Suresh is an emerging actress in South Indian movie industries. She was a child star in early 2000s and, after completing her graduation with a major in fashion designing, she returned back with the leading roles. In 2013, Keerthy Suresh played her first leading role in Malayalam movie titled Geethaanjali and has signed up a number of films in Telugu and Tamil languages as well.

Keerthy Suresh was born in Chennai, India on October 17, 1992. She was born to famous Malayali filmmaker Suresh Kumar and renowned actress Menaka. Keerthy has an elder sister named Revathy Suresh, who mainly works as a VFX expert and has formerly worked for Shah Rukh Khan’s production house called Red Chillies. Till class four, Keerthy Suresh completed her schooling in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.

She afterwards studied in Pattom, Kendriya Vidyalaya and Thiruvananthapuram prior returning back to Chennai with the intention of joining Pearl Academy wherein she completed her degree in fashion designing. Keerthy Suresh also spent time on a discussion program in Scotland for more than four months, prior completing her internship in London. In spite of making her career in acting, Keerthy has revealed that she was also earnestly considering a career in fashion designing.

In early 2000s, Keerthy Suresh appeared as a child star in numerous of her father’s productions like Achaneyanenikkishtam, Kuberan and Pilots. 11 years after Kuberan, Keerthy Suresh made her acting debut as a leading actress in Priyadarshan’s horror movie titled Geethaanjali, wherein she had a double role. Keerthy was still studying in that time and shot for Geethaanjali all through her semester interval.

In the year 2014, Keerthy Suresh had her next movie release titled Ring Master directed by Rafi Mecartin duo in wherein she appeared along with Dileep. In the same year, she chosen her first projects outside Malayalam and signed on to appear in a number of Tamil movie projects simultaneously.

Keerthy consequently worked in two movies with Sivakarthikeyan in rapid succession, Nurse Akka and Rajini Murugan, whilst also playing the leading role in Prabhu Solomon’s Rail together with Dhanush. In the same year, she also signed up her very first Telugu movie titled Aina Ishtam Nuvvu. Keerthy Suresh has completed her shooting for the artistic movie Darboni wherein she would be appeared as a Kashmiri girl.

Keerthy belongs to a Malayalam family with a filmy background as her father was a famous film producer and her Mom was a Tamil actress who had appeared in more than 100 movies.
