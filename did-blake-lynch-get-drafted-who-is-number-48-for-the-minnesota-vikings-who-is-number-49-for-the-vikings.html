Blake Lynch is an American football linebacker for the Minnesota Vikings of the National Football League.

Blake Lynch was born on February 14, 1997, in Gilmer, Texas.

Blake Lynch attended Gilmer High School.

After redshirting in his freshman year, Blake Lynch played college football for Baylor, where he made 32 career starts at five different positions, including; cornerback, linebacker, safety, wide receiver and running back.

In a four-year college career, Blake Lynch accrued 673 receiving yards, four receiving touchdowns and 79 rushing yards.

Blake Lynch played 13 snaps on special teams, and his only statistical contribution was a downed punt at the end of the Vikings’ first drive of the game.
